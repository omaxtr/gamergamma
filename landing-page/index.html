Common Questions

Is this a Color Calibration software?

No. Gamer Gamma is a Gamma Calibration and Tactical Advantage Utility. While professional color calibration focuses on hard-coded accuracy using expensive hardware, Gamer Gamma is about visual empowerment. It allows you to intentionally bend display logic to see enemies in the dark, reduce eye strain, or achieve a specific look that standard calibration might hide.

Will it affect my FPS?

Zero impact. Gamer Gamma uses low-level Windows APIs to communicate directly with your GPU's gamma ramp. It consumes virtually zero CPU or GPU resources while running in the background.

Will this get me banned?

No. It's Highly Unlikely Gamer Gamma modifies your display output at the OS level, not game files or memory. You can apply your settings, then close the application completely—the gamma adjustments persist without any background process. Anti-cheat systems have no way to detect display-level changes, as they occur outside the game environment entirely.
